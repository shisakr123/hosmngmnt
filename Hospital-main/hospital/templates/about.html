ABOUT IQRAA

The Iqraa International Hospital & Research Centre is planning to augment capacity from 250 beds to 600 beds. The hospital is located at Vengeri village in Kozhikode district of Kerala. The project comprises a cancer specialty block of basement+G+eight floors with modern amenities. Muhammad Abdu Yamani, whom we hold in high esteem, was the founder and mentor of our hospital.The objective of the hospital is to deliver high-quality healthcare services at affordable cost. Even though a charitable institution, IQRAA provides state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment facilities made affordable to people from all sections of society.

The hospital facilities provides strict and ethical medical practices, the hospital team ‐ which expands to 400 doctors and over 2500 nursing, paramedical and administrative staff – relentlessly strive to keep up our ideology, ‘ more than care ‘.
